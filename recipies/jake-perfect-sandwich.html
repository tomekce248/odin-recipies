Jake's Perfect Sandwich

A delicacy straight out of heaven, prepared by the master chef from The Land of OOO, Jake the Dog!!!

A rumor goes that whoever has the chance to taste this delicious sandwich, shall be granted the power to stretch their body all the way to the moon!!!

Ingredients

  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Bread
  • Cream Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Dill
  • Eggs
  • Bird Meat
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Yellow Onion (Organic)
  • Meat (Sous Vide)
  • Tears (For Salt)
  • Bacon
  • Lobster Soul

Steps

  1. Sharpen your knife 6 times.
  2. Wash your vegetables.
  3. Sous Vide your meat with tyhme and rosemary.
  4. Slice your bread in half, spread some cream cheese over the slices, add pickles and some dill.
  5. Boil and add your eggs (it is important for your eggs to be hard boiled).
  6. Chop your bird meat into little pieces until it looks like minced meat, then cook it in the pan.
  7. Add your cooked bird meat, slices of cucumbers, tomatoes and yellow onions.
  8. Add your tears after the contact with the yellow onions.
  9. Add your Sous Vide meat and cooked bacon.
  10. Boil your lobster and collect its soul as a gift from the gods of OOO.
  11. Add the lobster soul and put the top bun to create the Perfect Sandwich.

Voila, now your sandwich is ready, feel free to dig into it as a heavenly journey awaits you at the gates of OOO.
